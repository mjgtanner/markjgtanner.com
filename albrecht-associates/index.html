Albrecht + Associates

A fresh look on financial services

Albrecht + Associates is a financial services company with over 25 years of commercial experience. Hinged on combining technical rigor with a positive outlook and a genuine focus on business outcomes; Albrecht + Associates provides deep cost, compromise and risk insights that support a client-first approach. The founder Nik Albrecht has held positions ranging from General Counsel through to Head of Business Strategy, and he has various degrees including an MBA from INSEAD (Distinction). In his corporate life he worked at King & Wood Mallesons, UBS, Schroders and Wilsons Advisory.
